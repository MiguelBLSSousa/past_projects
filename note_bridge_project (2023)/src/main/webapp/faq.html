Where do the lessons happen?

Depending on the students and the teachers preference lessons can happen in 3 ways: at the teachers house, at the students house or online.

What is the price? How long are the lessons?

Note-Bridge offers lessons at a price range of 10-15 euros per hour. The duration of each lesson may vary depending on the teacher and the student's preferences, but typically they last for one hour.

Do I need an instrument?

No! At Note-Bridge our goal is to give everyone a chance to play music. If you wish to play an instrument you do not have, simply schedule the lesson at the teachers house.

Who are the teachers?

The teachers on Note-Bridge are experienced and qualified musicians who are passionate about music education. . Note-Bridge carefully selects and screens its teachers to ensure that they are knowledgeable, professional.

What if I need to cancel a lesson?

If you need to cancel or reschedule a lesson, don't worry. Simply let your teacher know as soon as possible, and they will work with you to find a new time that works for both of you. If you need to cancel a lesson completely, the money will be returned to you immediately.
